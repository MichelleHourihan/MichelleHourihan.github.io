"Sharing knowledge is the most fundamental act of friendship"

Daughter, mother, wife, friend, colleague, student, environmental activist, teacher, volunteer and developer are some of the words people may use to describe me. I have never found the label that can explain exactly who I am but like most of us, I want the world to be a better place and the future to be brighter. How? By working together and doing anything I can. I want to learn and share my knowledge, and help others do the same. I believe that both language and information are key to solving the world's problems.
